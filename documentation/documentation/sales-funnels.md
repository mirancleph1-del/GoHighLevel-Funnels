# GoHighLevel Sales Funnels

## Overview

A sales funnel guides potential customers from their first interaction with your business to becoming paying clients.

## Funnel Stages

1. Traffic Source
2. Landing Page
3. Lead Capture Form
4. Follow-up Automation
5. Appointment Booking
6. Sales Process
7. Customer Onboarding

## Best Practices

* Keep each page focused on one goal.
* Use compelling headlines and clear calls to action.
* Automate follow-up emails and SMS messages.
* Track conversions and optimize regularly.
