# GoHighLevel Landing Pages

## Overview

Landing pages are one of the most important parts of a successful GoHighLevel funnel. A well-designed landing page captures attention, builds trust, and encourages visitors to take action.

## Purpose

The primary goal of a landing page is to convert visitors into leads by focusing on a single offer or call-to-action.

## Essential Sections

* Hero section with a clear headline
* Value proposition
* Benefits
* Social proof or testimonials
* Lead capture form
* Call-to-action button
* Frequently Asked Questions
* Footer

## Best Practices

* Keep the design clean and focused.
* Use one primary call-to-action.
* Optimize for mobile devices.
* Reduce distractions and unnecessary links.
* Use fast-loading images.
* Test different headlines and button text.
