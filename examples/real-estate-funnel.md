# Real Estate Lead Generation Funnel

## Goal

Generate qualified buyer and seller leads and move them into an automated GoHighLevel follow-up system.

## Funnel Flow

1. Facebook or Instagram Ad
2. GoHighLevel Landing Page
3. Home Valuation or Property Inquiry Form
4. CRM Contact Created
5. Lead Added to Pipeline
6. Email and SMS Follow-Up
7. Appointment Booking
8. Sales Call or Consultation

## Automation

When a lead submits the form:

- A contact is created in GoHighLevel
- A tag is applied
- The lead is added to the real estate pipeline
- SMS confirmation is sent
- Email follow-up sequence begins
- Internal notification is sent to the agent
- Appointment reminder workflow starts

## Tools Used

- GoHighLevel Funnels
- GoHighLevel CRM
- GoHighLevel Workflows
- GoHighLevel Forms
- GoHighLevel Calendars
- Meta Ads
